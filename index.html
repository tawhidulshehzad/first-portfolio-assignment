The Future Picture

In order to reshape the country's future, Bangladesh 2.0 seeks to embrace innovative technology, encourage equitable growth, and guarantee sustainability. With efforts in digital transformation, renewable energy, and strong infrastructure, the nation is poised to become a global development model.

Digital Transformation

establishing a national digital ecosystem to improve service accessibility and connectivity for all residents.

Green Energy

investments in wind and solar power to guarantee a sustainable and environmentally responsible future.

Modern infrastructure

constructing cutting-edge infrastructure to boost global competitiveness and economic growth.
